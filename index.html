About Me

I'm passionate about robotics, AI, and creating innovative solutions. Currently pursuing my Master's in Robotics Engineering and working on projects that merge hardware and software to solve real-world problems.

My research focuses on autonomous navigation systems, machine learning applications in robotics, and human-robot interaction. I believe that the future of technology lies in creating intelligent systems that can seamlessly integrate into our daily lives.

When I'm not working on robotics projects, I enjoy contributing to open-source communities, writing about emerging technologies, and participating in robotics competitions.
