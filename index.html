Computer Vision & Mechatronics: Autonomous Drone System for Intralogistical Payload Transport

Pixhawk 4 Raspberry Pi 4 Computer Vision ArUco Markers PID Control 3D Printing

Developed an autonomous drone system for warehouse logistics that safely transports small payloads between locations. The system uses computer vision with ArUco markers for precise indoor localization, Pixhawk 4 for flight control, and custom 3D-printed gripping mechanisms. Capable of handling 2.4kg payloads with 6 minutes of flight time, demonstrating controlled flight and payload handling in constrained warehouse environments.

Industrial Robotics & Motion Control: Epson VT6 Drawing System with Precision Kinematics

Epson RC+ Robot Kinematics Motion Control Industrial Robotics Path Planning Calibration

Developed modular programs in Epson RC+ environment with TMove, Arc, CP, and Jump commands for smooth path execution. Implemented calibration procedures for tool alignment and 10mm approach offsets, demonstrating industrial robot manipulation and automation programming with high accuracy and repeatability.
