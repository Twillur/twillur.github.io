About Me

Passionate about creating intelligent systems that solve real-world problems

I'm a Robotics Engineer with a Master's degree in Robotics Engineering and a Bachelor's in Engineering Sciences from the American University of Armenia. My work focuses on the intersection of hardware and AI, creating systems that perceive, reason, and act autonomously.

From 2021 to 2025, I pursued a Bachelor of Engineering Sciences at AUA, gaining a strong foundation in advanced mathematics including numerical methods, ODEs, and calculus. My engineering coursework covered electricity and magnetism, circuits, signals and systems, dynamics, statics, and mechanics.

I've developed practical skills in computer-aided design (CAD), aerodynamics, and interdisciplinary engineering, providing me with a well-rounded understanding of both theoretical and applied engineering principles.
